Lemony Broccolini Pasta

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 4 servings
Ingredients Quantity
Casarecce 1 pound
Unsalted butter 1 tablespoon
Roughly chopped broccolini 1 pound
Thinly sliced garlic cloves 2
Lemon juice 1 lemon
Heavy cream ½ cup
Grated Parmesan cheese ⅓ cup
Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper 1
Pine nuts ½ cup
Chopped fresh basil 3 tablespoons

Step 1: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to the instructions on the package until al dente.

Step 2: Meanwhile, heat the olive oil and butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the broccolini and sauté until tender, about 5 minutes. Add the garlic, lemon zest and lemon juice; sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.

Step 3: Stir in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer over medium heat. Add the Parmesan cheese.

Step 4: Drain the pasta and then combine it with the sauce, tossing well to coat. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with pine nuts and basil.

One-Pan Roasted Chicken with Carrots

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4 servings
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken thighs 4
Peeled and trimmed carrots ½ pounds
Peeled and cut into eighths large onion, 1
Head of garlic 1
Olive oil 4 tablespoons
Chopped fresh rosemary 1 tablespoon
Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper 1

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 425°F.

Step 2: Arrange the carrots and onion in a single layer on a greased baking sheet.

Step 3: Slice the top off a head of garlic; discard the top and place on the tray.

Step 4: Drizzle 2 tablespoons of olive oil over the vegetables; season with the rosemary, and salt and pepper.

Step 5: Top with the chicken thighs. Rub each thigh with 1 teaspoon olive oil; season with salt and pepper.

Step 6: Roast in the oven until the chicken skin is golden and the carrots are tender, 15 to 20 minutes.

Step 7: To serve, divide the vegetables and chicken thighs among four plates.

Ravioli Lasagna

  • Time: 1 hour 25 minutes
  • Serves: 8 servings
Ingredients Quantity
Olive oil 2 tablespoons
Diced sweet onion 1
Minced garlic cloves 2
Ground beef 1 pound
Salt 1 teaspoon
Freshly ground black pepper ¾ teaspoon
Dried oregano 1 teaspoon
Marinara sauce 1½ cups
Ravioli 2 pounds
Ricotta cheese 1½ cups
Shredded mozzarella cheese 2 cups
Chopped fresh basil 3 tablespoons

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 375°F. Lightly grease a 9-by-13-inch casserole dish with nonstick spray.

Step 2: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and sauté until translucent, 4 to 5 minutes. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant, 1 minute more.

Step 3: Add the ground beef and sauté until fully cooked, 6 to 8 minutes. Season with the salt, pepper and oregano.

Step 4: Stir in the marinara sauce and bring to a simmer over medium-low heat. Simmer until the sauce thickens slightly and has good flavor, 20 minutes.

Step 5: While the sauce cooks, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. When it comes to a rolling boil, add the ravioli and cook according to the instructions on the package. Drain well.

Step 6: To build the lasagna, place a layer of ravioli in the base of the pan. Ladle a quarter of the sauce over the ravioli, then top with ½ cup ricotta cheese, spread into a layer. Sprinkle with a ¼ cup mozzarella.

Step 7: Repeat the layering process two more times. For the fourth and final layer, add one more layer of ravioli, sauce and mozzarella (no ricotta this time).

Step 8: Bake the lasagna until the sauce is bubbly and the cheese is melty, 25 to 30 minutes. Let cool at least 10 minutes before slicing and serving. Garnish with basil and serve warm.
